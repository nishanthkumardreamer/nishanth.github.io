Title: Autonomous Mobile Robot

Description: The goal of the project is to develop an autonomous vehicle. To develop a self driving robot that moves to the desired location when the location is fed to the vehicle.To build a robot that can detect and avoids obstacles in its path in reaching the destination location. Time to reach destination is compromised as the main factor is to reach without dashing with obstacles.The vehicle can receive the destination location sent from mobile. It can detect obstacles before it and finds an alternate path to the destination. It reaches the destination without dashing but consumes time to reach the destination.
